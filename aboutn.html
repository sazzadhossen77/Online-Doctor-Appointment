At JustDoc, we believe that everyone should have instant and affordable access to a board-certified doctor, whenever and wherever needed. The lack of access to affordable healthcare is a large and growing problem, making our mission that much more important. We can connect anyone – with or without insurance – to high-quality physicians, saving valuable time and money. Hundreds of employers and patient trust JustDoc to provide care to their tens of millions of employees and members. Unlike many healthcare websites, we do not promote any sponsored doctors or show a locally available doctor who might have a vested interest to earn more from medicines and diagnostic labs. When you are consulting a doctor on JustDoc you are getting an unbiased solution so that you can make an informed decision.
We work hard to make sure that your information remains anonymous and secure. Your trust is important to us so we don't identify individuals in our database. We do ask for personal information on the website so that we can connect you to the best doctor.
